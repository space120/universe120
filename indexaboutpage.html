About Universe120

Universe120 is a digital blueprint tool that reimagines how people learn, design, and create—built on a 2D bubble graph that turns lines, curves, and intersections into a playful, precise way to understand space.

Who It Serves

Students & Teachers: Explore geometry by building it. Draw triangles, tessellations, and proofs; see angles, symmetry, and distance relationships come to life. Use it for live demos and guided activities.

Architects & Designers: Rapidly sketch concepts with blueprint clarity. The grid’s snap points keep layouts clean and proportional so you can focus on ideas, not fighting a tool.

How It Works

The 2D bubble graph uses evenly spaced overlapping circles. Wherever circles meet, you get a precise snap point. Those points act like magnetic studs for lines and curves—ideal for straight segments, arcs, and clean intersections.

  • 2D Mode: Build structures, rooms, and geometric models with snap guidance.
  • 3D & 4D (Coming Soon): See how shapes evolve into higher dimensions through step-by-step construction.
  • Blueprint Theme: White “ink” on deep blue for a familiar plan-drawing feel.

Our Mission

To make spatial design and mathematical visualization simple, hands-on, and inspiring—for every learner, creator, and thinker. Universe120 bridges art, architecture, and education so anyone can build ideas with confidence.
