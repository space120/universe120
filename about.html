About Universe120

Draw on a bubble-grid with smart snap/angles, place openings, then preview simple 3D walls and export PNG/SVG/glTF. Runs fully in your browser during beta.

What’s included

  • 2D bubble-grid editor with tools + smart guides
  • “Opening” rectangles become holes when extruded
  • 3D viewer (orbit & presets, wall height, wireframe)
  • Export: PNG/SVG (2D) and glTF (3D)
  • Move/rotate/select/group/ungroup
  • Units selector + scale bar
