About Universe120

Universe120 is a blueprint-style drawing playground where people learn how our world grows from 2D lines to 3D shapes—and how imagination helps us sense a 4th direction.

What it is

We start with a friendly bubble grid (overlapping circles that feel like graph paper). From there, you can draw precisely with smart guides, snap to intersections, set angles, and export your work. The goal is fast visual learning that’s accurate enough for classrooms and concept sketches.

  • 2D Bubble Grid: symmetry, intersections, equal lengths, parallels.
  • Smart Guides: angle hints (0/30/45/60/90°), equal-length, parallel/perpendicular badges.
  • Export: PNG for sharing, SVG for maker/laser workflows and classroom printables.

Who it’s for

Curious minds ages 6–106. It’s kid-friendly, but powerful enough for teens, teachers, and architects doing rough concept work.

Accessibility & privacy

Universe120 runs completely in your browser. We don’t collect personal data or require sign-ups in the beta. A high-contrast mode and keyboard-only controls are on the roadmap.
