About Universe120

Universe120 is a digital blueprint tool that reimagines how people learn, design, and create. Built around a 2D bubble graph, it turns lines, curves, and intersections into a playful yet powerful way to understand space and structure.

Who It Serves

Students & Teachers: Learn geometry visually by building it. Every shape shows the math behind it, helping students see symmetry, angles, and proportion.

Architects & Designers: Quickly sketch floor plans, structures, and ideas that snap perfectly into place — no CAD software required.

How It Works

The 2D bubble graph replaces square grids with overlapping circles. Every intersection acts as a snap point — a magnetic guide for straight lines, arcs, and curves. It’s geometry you can see and feel.

  • 2D Mode: Build structures and designs with perfect alignment.
  • 3D & 4D (Coming Soon): Explore how shapes evolve in higher dimensions.
  • Blueprint Theme: Classic white-on-blue visuals for a professional feel.

Our Mission

To make spatial design and math visualization simple, hands-on, and inspiring for every learner and creator. Universe120 bridges art, architecture, and education to help anyone build their imagination into form.
