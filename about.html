About Universe120

Universe120 is a digital blueprint tool that helps students, teachers, and architects build ideas with precision — starting from a 2D bubble grid.

What it is

The 2D bubble graph is a grid of evenly spaced, overlapping circles. Where circles touch, you get perfectly repeatable snap-points — magnetic anchors for straight lines, arcs, and clean intersections. It’s a friendlier alternative to square graph paper for geometry and planning.

  • 2D Mode: Construct rooms, patterns, and geometric models with snap guidance.
  • 3D & 4D (Coming Soon): Learn how shapes evolve to higher dimensions via interactive builds.
  • Blueprint Theme: White “ink” on deep blue — easy to read on projectors and in classrooms.

Who it serves

Students & Teachers: See math by making it — symmetry, 30/60/90 directions, tessellations, and distance. Use it for demos, activities, and printable exports.

Architects & Designers: Rapid, precise concept sketching. Walls snap; doors and arcs align; labels and simple dimensions keep things clear.

Mission

Make spatial design and mathematical thinking simple, hands-on, and inspiring for every learner and creator. Universe120 bridges art, architecture, and education — so anyone can turn imagination into form.
