Help & Shortcuts

Navigation

  • Pan: hold Space and drag
  • Zoom: mouse wheel (zooms to cursor)
  • Zoom Fit: Shift+1 • Reset 100%: 1

Tools

  • Select (V): click to select; Shift+click to add; drag a box to multi-select. Move selection by dragging; rotate using the yellow handle.
  • Line (L): click–click to draw edges. Smart guides show equal length, parallel, perpendicular, and “nice” angles (0/30/45/60/90°).
  • Pen (P): chained lines (polylines). Enter to finish; Backspace to undo a point.
  • Rect (R), Circle (O), Arc (A): shape primitives for fast layout.
  • Text (T): labels; Dim (D): measurement line with length.
  • Erase (E): delete nearest element.

Snapping: use the toolbar “Snap” menu (Intersections / Centers / Midpoints / Free) and “Angle” menu for 0/90°, 30/60/90°, or 45/90° locks.

Groups

  • Select multiple → Group (button or G) → move/rotate as one.
  • Ungroup with button or U.

Uploading & Auto-Aligning Blueprints

  1. Click Upload Blueprint (top bar) and choose an image (PNG/JPG).
  2. Follow the status prompt:
    • Click Point 1 on the image (a corner or door center).
    • Then click the matching grid point (it will snap to the graph).
    • Repeat for Point 2 image → Point 2 grid.
    The app computes the best rotation/scale/position so the plan sits perfectly on the grid.
  3. Use the Blueprint Opacity slider or Lock toggle to prevent accidental moves.
  4. Trace, add walls, annotate, or export SVG/PNG.

Export

Use Export SVG for vector CAD-friendly output, or Export PNG for screenshots.
