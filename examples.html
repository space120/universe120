Examples

These blueprint-style demos show how real plans would look snapped onto the Universe120 bubble grid. They’re simplified outlines created with straight lines, arcs, and circles—exportable as SVG or PNG.

Golden Gate Bridge — snapped truss & towers

Aligned to the bubble grid (hex-packed circles). The towers, suspenders, and deck lines “land” on intersections like they’d been drawn with the Line + Angle Lock. Simplified for demo purposes.

Disney World — hub & spokes + zones

A simplified “hub and spokes” concept map snapped to the grid. In the app, an architect/teacher could upload a plan, auto-align it to intersections, trace/edit, and export.
